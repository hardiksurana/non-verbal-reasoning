Non-Verbal Reasoning Quiz

Welcome! You are a participant in a live research study being conducted to evaluate non-verbal reasoning questions

Disclaimer : We do not store any of your personal data. The results are collected and stored anonymously and used solely for research purposes.

Kindly follow the following instructions for each attempt of the quiz:

Multiple attempts are severely encouraged!
  1. Login with your email address
    • If you have logged in for the first time, you will be prompted to enter a few basic details (Again, only for research purposes!)
  2. Click on the 'Take New Quiz' button
  3. You will be presented with a multiple-choice question with 4 answer options
  4. Attempt the question by choosing the answer you think is right
  5. For each question, you will be asked to fill some feedback. Please provide your honest feedback to help us improve.
  6. Submit the quiz
  7. You can view your score at the end of the quiz

You will attempt the following question types:

Cut

A figure is given and a part of it is cut out. You have to identify which of the options completes the figure.

Dice

An open cube figure is given. You have to identify which of the options best describes a cube made out of the given paper piece.

Fold

An unfolded figure is given. You have to identify how the pattern would appear when the paper is folded on the dotted line.


Figure Matrix and Sequence

A matrix of blocks are given, each of which follow a pattern. You have to identify which of the options continues the pattern.

Grid

A variety of patterns are given as a grid. You have to identify which of the options follows the pattern.

Series

A series of figures are given which follow a certain pattern of transformation. You have to identify which of the options is the next figure in the series.
